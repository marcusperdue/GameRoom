Working

Quick setup

Get playing in three steps

1 Add games

Use Add Games or drag files in. For ISO files, pick the console first.

2 Fix yellow setup items

Open the shown folder and add the required files from your own hardware.

3 Press Play

GameRoom launches the right emulator and backs up saves first.

GameRoom can use emulator apps you download or install. It does not include games or BIOS files. Use your own legally dumped games and console files.
